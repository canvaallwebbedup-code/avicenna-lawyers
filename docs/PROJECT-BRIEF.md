## The client
Avicenna Lawyers — boutique law firm, Merrylands NSW (Western Sydney). Founded by two
solicitors with 30+ years combined experience; also a newer solicitor, Mona. Broad
services: commercial, corporate, civil, criminal, traffic, immigration, wills & estates,
employment, property & conveyancing, consumer law.

Multilingual — English, Farsi, Dari, Arabic, Urdu, Hindi, Pashto, Spanish. A genuine
point of difference; reflect it in the design where appropriate.

Operates from a shared Merrylands office also home to a travel agency and a
remittance/money-transfer business run by the same family. Community-facing, serves a
diverse, multicultural Western Sydney client base. (Internal context for tone — NOT page copy.)

## Positioning
- Boutique, NOT a volume legal factory. Personalised service, long-term relationships.
- Corporate/commercial is the backbone — repeat clients, ongoing relationships.
- Criminal/traffic complements it, but the firm does NOT want to be seen primarily as a
  criminal law firm.
- Frame: a trusted local firm that handles serious commercial matters and also looks
  after clients when things go wrong personally.

## Why these pages exist
Two Google Ads landing pages, one per practice area. Single job: convert an ad click into
an enquiry (call or form). Every element — headline, layout, copy, CTA — serves conversion.
Format reference: https://www.maxhaulage.au/

## Page 1 — Criminal & Traffic
Audience: just charged with a criminal/traffic offence, or facing a Local Court hearing.
Stressed, possibly embarrassed, urgent — not browsing.
Tone: reassuring, discreet, calm, competent. Empathy and trust; not hard-sell, boastful,
or aggressive. Within seconds: "I'm in the right place, and calling is easy and safe."
HARD CONSTRAINT — Local Court work ONLY. Must NOT reference District Court, serious
criminal matters, high-profile cases, or custodial framing. No association with serious
crime, given the firm's corporate client base and office environment.

## Page 2 — Corporate & Commercial
Audience: business owners, directors, individuals with a commercial dispute or needing
corporate advice. Considered; may be comparing firms. Want credibility, expertise, a firm
that understands business.
Tone: sharp, professional, commercially minded. More confident/direct than Page 1.
Responds to expertise and track record, not warmth. Emphasise long-term relationships —
the firm business owners come back to, not one-off work.

## Hard constraints / non-negotiables
1. Criminal page = Local Court only. No District Court / serious crime / high-profile / custodial.
2. Never position the firm primarily as a criminal practice.
3. Boutique, personalised, relationship-driven — never "volume", "cheap", "factory".
4. No guaranteed-outcome language (NSW legal advertising rules). No "win / beat the charge / get off".
5. Multilingual (8 languages) is a real differentiator — surface on both pages.
6. Don't invent facts, stats, credentials, or reviews.
