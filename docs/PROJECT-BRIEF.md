## Page 1 — Criminal & Traffic
Audience: just charged with a criminal or traffic offence, or facing a court date.
Stressed, possibly embarrassed, urgent — not browsing.
Tone: reassuring, discreet, calm, competent. Empathy and trust; not hard-sell, boastful,
or aggressive. Within seconds: "I'm in the right place, and calling is easy and safe."
SCOPE (updated 2026-06) — present the SPECIFIC matter types the firm handles via the two
on-page lists: "Criminal charges we handle" and "Driving & traffic matters we handle". Do
NOT use "Local Court" or any court-level label (no Local/District Court framing). Do NOT
imply the firm only does minor/first-time matters, or that it can't act on more serious
charges where appropriate. Equally, do NOT market serious / high-profile / indictable /
custodial criminal defence — keep it discreet, calm and reassuring. No guaranteed-outcome
language. Free initial PHONE consultation is confirmed for CRIMINAL matters — state it on
this page only.

## Page 2 — Corporate & Commercial
Audience: business owners, directors, individuals with a commercial dispute or needing
corporate advice. Considered; may be comparing firms. Want credibility, expertise, a firm
that understands business.
Tone: sharp, professional, commercially minded. More confident/direct than Page 1.
Responds to expertise and track record, not warmth. Emphasise long-term relationships —
the firm business owners come back to, not one-off work.
SERVICES (updated 2026-06) — present the firm's full commercial scope under two on-page
headings: "Commercial Litigation" and "Commercial General" (see the page for the exact
item lists). Languages are NOT surfaced on this page (removed per client).

## Hard constraints / non-negotiables
1. Criminal page: present the specific matter types listed on the page. No court-level labels
   ("Local Court" / District Court). Don't imply minor-only or can't-do-serious; equally don't
   market serious / high-profile / indictable / custodial defence. Keep it discreet, calm, reassuring.
2. Never position the firm primarily as a criminal practice.
3. Boutique, personalised, relationship-driven — never "volume", "cheap", "factory".
4. No guaranteed-outcome language (NSW legal advertising rules). No "win / beat the charge / get off".
5. Multilingual (8 languages) is a real differentiator — surface on the CRIMINAL page ONLY
   (removed from the corporate page per client).
6. Don't invent facts, stats, credentials, or reviews.
